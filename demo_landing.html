Today's question

Which charity should receive a $10 donation?

The situation

In this study, you'll make a collective choice about where a small donation should go. Several charities are working on different approaches to addressing social problems. Each of them uses donations in different ways and focuses on different types of impact.

In an anonymous chat with a neutral AI assistant, you'll rank the options and briefly explain why exactly you prefer your top choice. After everyone in the group makes their choice, the charity that receives the most support will be selected as the winner.

Before making your decision, please read the descriptions of the charities carefully.

As you read, try to think about:

  • what effects the donation could have
  • which problems you think are most important to address
  • why you think one option may be better than the others

There are no right or wrong answers. We are interested in how people think about these choices.

Charity options at a glance

Community Animal Rescue Shelter

A nonprofit animal rescue organization that takes in abandoned or neglected dogs and cats in New York City. The shelter provides food, medical care, behavioral support, and temporary housing while working to find permanent adoptive homes. Donations help cover veterinary treatment, shelter operations, and adoption programs that give homeless animals a chance to live in safe, long-term homes.

Donations support food distribution, transportation, and partnerships with local organizations that provide meals and groceries to families experiencing hunger.

Urban Tree Initiative

A nonprofit that works with communities across New York City to plant and care for street trees and other urban green spaces. The organization trains volunteers and local residents to maintain trees, organizes planting projects, and promotes stewardship of the city’s urban forest. Donations help fund tree planting, maintenance programs, and environmental education for residents and youth.

How today's session works

Phase 1

Share your view

You'll have a private conversation with an AI moderator. It will ask for a full ranking across the four charities and a short reason for your top choice.

Between phases

Ideas are collected

Once everyone has responded, the reasoning from all participants is anonymised and neutrally rephrased — no names, no attribution.

Phase 2

Hear others' thinking

You'll return to the chat to read others' ideas and then restate your final ranking and reasoning after that reflection.

Outcome

Collective picture

We can compare initial and final rankings to see how views shifted (or stayed stable) without direct debate.
