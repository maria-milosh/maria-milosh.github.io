MetaGov Holiday Season Planning Discussion

As the year draws to a close, we’re inviting members of the MetaGov community to share their ideas for a holiday season activity. What kinds of gatherings, events, or reflections could help us celebrate and strengthen our community?

This survey is hosted on Harmonica, a conversational survey platform. You’ll chat with AI for around 10 minutes to share your ideas about holiday activities.

Please note: this is a solicitation of ideas and community input, not a commitment from MetaGov to implement specific activities.

Thank you for contributing!
